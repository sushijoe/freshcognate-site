About Freshcognate

We're a boutique shop of creators and strategists at the intersection of education, public health, communications, technology, curriculum writing, and video production located in Somerville, Mass. We represent decades of experience providing solutions for new and unique problems.
We are passionate about the work we do and strive to build long-term partnerships with companies and institutions to connect communities with solutions and new ways of thinking and push the boundaries of educational design and communication.
