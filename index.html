A brand for a company is like a reputation for a person. You earn reputation by trying to do hard things well.

We're passionate about leveraging education and technology to connect the world. The terrain of online education is constantly-changing and challenging. We can help you sort through the myriad options and develop resources that will stand the test of time. We specialize in scaling educational resources -- online and in the classroom.
We take a holistic approach to educational design: curriculum design, video production, proven pedagogical methods and a thoughtful iterative approach. We love a challenge and will custom tailor courses to fit the most difficult and hard to deliver subjects.

Graduates of the Harvard Graduate School of Education and the MIT media lab, with clients ranging from Mckinsey & co. to MIT we remain embedded in a rich community, constantly pushing the boundaries of educational design.
