TeachLab Podcast

Podcast production/development + Video Production

Home • Portfolio • MIT CSAIL

TeachLab with Justin Reich is a podcast that explores the art and craft of teaching. Aimee Corrigan, FC’s Chief of Creative, has served as TeachLab’s Co-Producer in collaboration with Producer Garrett Beazley and Executive Producer and Host, Justin Reich.

From ideation to launch, through research and development, and production and release of 4 seasons of episodes, we’ve crafted a dynamic and engaging podcast for an audience of K-12 educators and people who are passionate about education in the US and around the world. We’ve interviewed world class luminaries like Dr. Beverly Daniel Tatum and Geoffrey Canada, spotlighted the work of organizations like pEN America and Southern Poverty Law Center, and most importantly, told the stories of dozens of incredible educators.. In addition to research, creative direction and audio production, our team has assisted in video production and marketing efforts for TeachLab.

Check out TeachLab’s most recent series of episodes, "Teacher Speech and the New Divide" We’re talking with attorneys and legal scholars about the history of freedom of speech for teachers, hearing stories from educators across the country who’ve come up against divisive concept laws and book bans, and providing insights to help educators navigate this shifting landscape.
